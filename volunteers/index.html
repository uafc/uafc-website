Join as a volunteer.

Do you have a passion for engineering and technology? Sign up on our form and we will reach out to chat with you. Even if you've never built a technology project before, no need to worry! We provide all the training and guidance you need. For more challenging projects, you will also have access to our University of Alberta Engineering Faculty partners for support.

We welcome students outside the engineering faculty as well! We have roles including everything from outreach and marketing to finance and business, and we'd love to have you on our team.


What Do Volunteers Do?
