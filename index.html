Empowering youth to create their own technological project.

What we do.

Make a difference!

Future Creators has mentored over 200 grade 7-12 students from all across Canada, helping them build everything from solar chargers to Bluetooth-controlled robot cars to machine learning video games for free. Join our program, and you'll also have a shot at the Innovation Awards—cash prizes given out each mentorship round to three mentees who can make the most innovative additions onto their project! Join us on our mission to help empower young innovators find their passion for technology.
